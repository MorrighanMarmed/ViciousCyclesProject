7 WAYS TO PREPARE FOR COMPETITION

April 5, 2022

Competitions are a great way to test your skills and find out where you stand in the industry. They can also be a great way to get your name out there and build a reputation for yourself. There are some things that you should do before competing so that you can be ready to go when the time comes. Here are 7 ways to prepare for competitions: 1) Training: The best way to prepare is by training, training, and more training. You should train with different types of endurance levels and formats so that you know what's expected of you when it's time for the real deal. 2) Know Yourself: You need to know your abilities and limits and what goals you want to reach. 3) Find a mentor: If you have someone who has competed before, they can offer advice and help you prepare for the competition. 4) Rest: This is important to rebuild lost energy. 5) Nutrition: Getting the best food for your body will help with training and endurance. 6) Join a club or organization: Joining clubs or organizations that are related to your field will give you an idea of what is involved. 7) Find out what type of cycling matches your abilities best (road, mountain, cyclocross, etc.)
